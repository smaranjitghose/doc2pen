What?

DOC2PEN is a student's saviour to submit the assignments by just typing them out and getting it handwritten on the go! It simply converts image to handwritten document and and also allow us to download documents easily.

Why?

When we peep into the life of a student, each and every student has to write many assignments during their schools, and colleges, and it consumes a lot of time to write the assignments, and sometimes writing is painful. Nowadays, people tend to type faster than they can write. Typing is better if we need to write a lot, and almost everyone likes to type which is not only faster but increasingly economical in number of wonderful ways. DOC2PEN has been developed to help students to just type out assignments and change those in handwritten documents in just one go.
