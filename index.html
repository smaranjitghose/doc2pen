What are we about?

One day,   Smitha   was very tensed about the assignments she has to submit in her college, because   She Can Type Fast, But Can't Write Fast.

On talking about this issue to her friend Harry, he suggested Doc2Pen as a solution to her problem.

Are you facing the same problem as Smitha did?

So, let's hear what Harry says about Doc2Pen.


Doc2Pen converts your typed assignments into handwritten ones.

Step 1 : Pick a Page


Here, either you can choose one from the wide variety of pages available or you can upload your own institution's letterpad.


Step 2 : Pick a Font


You can adjust the combination of font-style, font-color and font-size as the one which resembles your handwriting.


Step 3 : Type it


Use your fast typing skill to type the assignment in the editor and get it in the handwritten form.


Step 4 : Download it


You can now download/export it in the .png, .jpg, .jpeg or .pdf format and get it printed in order to submit in the college.


Now, as Doc2Pen saves a lot of your time, you can now spent your valuable time in learning some practical stuffs.

And So, here comes the Good News, Doc2pen is an Open Source Project, we welcome your contributions.🙂
